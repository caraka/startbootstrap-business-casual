Welcome to

Keep Auckland Beautiful

A city that's both liveable and tidy

The Keep Auckland Beautiful Trust (KAB) was established in 2009 in anticipation of the city's amalgamation and the need for regional approaches to litter and illegal dumping. KAB is a member branch of the Keep New Zealand Beautiful Society.

Currently KAB is a member of and the delivery arm for the Auckland Litter Prevention Steering Group, or ALPSG.

The ALPSG was re-constituted in 2013 with the express purpose of coordinating a regional approach to litter and illegal dumping. The ALPSG members are all the major asset holders within the Auckland region, including Council Parks, Council Waste Solutions, the New Zealand Transport Agency, KiwiRail and the Auckland Motorway Alliance.

We are half way through a 3 year project which includes a re-fresh of the Be A Tidy Kiwi identity. We are currently piloting our 3 final designs in cinemas, schools and parks across Auckland and beyond.

About Be A Tidy Kiwi

Tidy Kiwi (TK) was born in the 1970s at a time when tossing your takeaways out the car window was the normal thing to do. Kiwis and tourists alike embraced TK - unifying all of us as kaitiaki (guardians) - and giving birth to New Zealand's clean green identity. TK has had several incarnations over the years, but nothing definitive in over a decade.

One of the ALPSG's objectives is to revitalise TK so that a new generation of New Zealanders can discover this iconic identity and come together to protect what we love. We're a collection of central and local government funders on a three year mission to bring TK back to life.

Will you helps us? We're getting close to choosing a new identity for TK and we'd like your opinion. We have a brief on line survey and would appreciate your feedback and sharing with your networks. It's at www.beatidykiwi.nz.
